About our company

Welcome to Abraham & Kurian, Chartered Accountants—your trusted financial advisors. Since 1994, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality professional services in audit and assurance, taxation, business restructuring, valuations, and regulatory compliance. Backed by a team of experienced Chartered Accountants, we are committed to providing tailored financial solutions that support your business growth.

The story behind how our company was founded

Founded in November 1994, Abraham & Kurian is a leading Chartered Accountancy firm headquartered in Thiruvalla, Pathanamthitta. The firm is led by a distinguished team of partners— CA Antony Francis, CA M S Venkitachalam and CA Premji Pillai —who bring extensive expertise and a commitment to professional excellence. Since its inception, we have delivered a wide range of financial and legal services, serving a diverse clientele with integrity, precision, and personalized solutions.

Our vision

To be recognized as one of the most trusted and respected CA firms in Central Kerala, consistently delivering value through professionalism, excellence, and client satisfaction.

We aim to foster long-term relationships built on trust, while also nurturing a positive and growth-oriented environment for our team—ensuring that we deliver our best to every client, every time.

Our mission

Our mission is to empower our clients by delivering strategic, insightful, and result-driven financial services. We are committed to long-term partnerships, helping our clients enhance corporate value, navigate challenges, and achieve sustainable growth in a dynamic business environment.

Through a combination of expertise, integrity, and innovation, Abraham & Kurian strives to be a key driver in our clients' financial success. We maintain the highest standards of professional excellence while providing clarity that enables confident decision-making.

Our Approach

At Abraham & Kurian, we believe in building meaningful partnerships through a consultative, client-first approach. Our process begins by understanding your business goals, followed by identifying your specific financial needs and delivering customised, practical solutions.

We work with a diverse clientele across sectors such as trading, non-profits (NGOs), banking, and financial services, ensuring that each client receives industry-specific insights and strategies. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and continuous improvement ensures that we uphold the highest standards in everything we do.
