Staying compliant with ever-changing regulatory requirements is crucial for business continuity and growth. At Abraham and Kurian, we ensure your company adheres to all statutory norms, from MCA filings to comprehensive internal and tax audits, providing peace of mind and regulatory confidence.

Our Complete Compliance & Audit Services

Financial Statement Audit

We conduct comprehensive audits in accordance with Indian Accounting Standards and Statements on Auditing (SAs) to ensure financial accuracy, transparency, and regulatory compliance for your organization.

Internal Audit

Our internal audit services evaluate operational efficiency, identify potential risks, and help streamline business processes while ensuring robust internal controls and governance frameworks.

Tax Audit

Our comprehensive tax audits ensure accurate filing under the Income Tax Act, help identify potential issues early, and mitigate penalties while optimizing your tax position.

MCA Compliance

We provide complete assistance with ROC filings, annual returns, board resolutions, and all compliance documentation required under the Companies Act and other applicable regulations.
