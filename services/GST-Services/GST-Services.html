GST Consultation

The introduction of GST on 1st July 2017 revolutionized the indirect taxation system in India by consolidating multiple central and state taxes under a single framework. As leading GST consultants in Thiruvalla, Abraham and Kurian helps businesses navigate this complex and evolving tax structure.

We offer comprehensive GST services including return filing, rate classification, audits, and advisory — ensuring complete compliance and minimal business disruption.

Our GST Services
  • GST Return Filing
    Our GST experts in Thiruvalla assist with the preparation and timely filing of GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and other relevant forms under GSTIN.
  • Tax Rate Classification
    We ensure correct classification of goods and services under IGST, CGST, and SGST slabs and keep your tax liability optimized and compliant.
  • Vendor Reconciliation
    Our team validates purchase and sales invoices to detect mismatches and maintain ITC integrity under GST.
  • Compliance and Audit Support
    We conduct ongoing GST audits, advance ruling support, and assist startups in navigating GST implications during their growth.
